About Flow.

THE VISION

As modern technology continues to innovate and advance, it has never been easier to achieve and attain one's goals. And yet, the increasing influence of social media and numerous other distractions has made it harder to execute upon these ideas with focus. Thus, Flow was made for everyone - students, employees, individuals working toward their goals - to eliminate external online distractions and help their focus.

THE PRODUCT

As of now, Flow is a free Chrome Extension that can be easily installed from the Chrome Web Store. However, Flow in the future will also become a mobile app for everyone - iOS and Android users - to install and eliminate distractions on their mobile devices.
